About

Diverdle is a daily guessing game where you try to identify the day's featured weapon from Arrowhead's hit title, Helldivers II. Released in February 2024, Helldivers II has quickly become a fan favorite among gamers worldwide. Now it's your turn to put your knowledge of its vast arsenal to the test with Diverdle!

How To Play

To play Diverdle, simply type in the name of a weapon and it's properties will be revealed. The color of the boxes will show how close your guessed weapon was to the weapon of the day.

Green indicates that the weapon shares a common property with the weapon of the day.

Yellow indicates that the properties partially match.

Red indicates that the properties do not match. If the property is numerical, there will also be an up or down arrow to indicate whether the value for the weapon of the day is higher or lower.

Types of Properties


Weapon Category:

This is the in-game category that the weapon falls under.

ex: Primary, Secondary, Support

Weapon Type:

This is the class that the weapon falls under.

ex: Assualt Rifle, Shotgun, Energy-Based, Anti-Tank

Damage:

This is the amount of damage that the weapon does per shot.

This value will range anywhere from ~50 - upwards of 2500

Armor Penetration:

This is the level of Armor Penetration that the weapon has.

This value will be between 2(Light) - 7(Anti-Tank III)

Traits:

These are the traits that define the weapon.

ex: Explosive, One Handed, Incendiary, Rounds Reload, Stationary Reload
